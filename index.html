The Role of a Professional School Counselor

Professional school counselors support student success across three main domains: academic development, social-emotional growth, and college and career readiness. They collaborate with teachers, administrators, and families to ensure all students receive equitable support and opportunities to thrive.

  • Academic Development: helping students build study skills, improve organization, manage their time, and set academic goals.
  • Social-Emotional Support: assisting students with emotional regulation, conflict resolution, stress management, and peer relationship skills.
  • College & Career Readiness: guiding students in exploring careers, planning for college, understanding financial aid, and preparing for future success.
